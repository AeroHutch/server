# Server Rules

## WARNING
If you do any of these you could get a ban depending on the serverity and reason

## Money Rules
#### 1. Pay all your employees equally
 - If you do not do this you will get your shop taken down and your permit handed to someone else
#### 2. You will get paying money from the government
 - If you are short the government is forced to pay you a given amount to give to all your employees
## Property Rules
#### 1. No Ruining other builds
 - No TNT
 - No Breaking Blocks
 - No Ruining Builds
 - If it was built by someone (even if it is not claimed. it's not fun to destroy players builds)
#### 2. No Illegal Tresspassing
 - Do not enter without paying (if it requires payment)
 - Do not enter without permission (first ask then act accordingly)
 - Do not enter when banned (could get you banned from the server. It depends)
 - Do not enter without a permit (like building sites, *government buildings ect.)
#### 3. No Illegal Building
 - Building in Government Land without a permit
 - Building without permission
 - Building in Claims
 - (Tip: Always request a plot to build on)
#### 4. No Copying Builds
 - Copying a build 1:1 (please only take the idea and don't copy it exactly)
 - Making the build not unique enough
